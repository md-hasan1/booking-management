# Booking Management System

**Project:** [App Link](#) || [Backend-Code](https://github.com)

---

## 📝 Project Description

A comprehensive **booking and appointment management backend** connecting service specialists with customers. Enables seamless appointment scheduling, specialist discovery, secure payments, and real-time notifications for a service-based business ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Features

- **Service Discovery & Booking**: Browse and search services by category, schedule appointments with specialists, manage availability and time slots
- **Payments & Subscriptions**: Secure payment processing with PayFast, subscription management, earnings tracking, invoicing, and payout management
- **Notifications & Content**: Email notifications via Brevo, Firebase push notifications, reviews/ratings system, favorites, search history, and admin content moderation
